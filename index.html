Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs your use of the software application KVP Launcher for mobile devices. We created this application as a free software service to provide a simple and accessible interface for users.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our application requires certain sensitive permissions to provide the core functionality of the launcher. We do not use these permissions to collect your personal information. They are used exclusively on your local device for the following purposes:

  • Notification Access: Used strictly to display the count of unread messages and notifications directly on the home screen icons.
  • Phone State and Call Logs: Used exclusively to detect and display the number of missed calls on the home screen interface.

2. Third-Party Data Transfer

We do not collect, store, track, or transfer any of your personal data, message contents, notification details, or call history to external servers or third-party companies. All operations are processed entirely on your physical device and never leave it.

3. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes become effective immediately upon being posted on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any updates.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer.
