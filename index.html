Privacy Policy for KVP Launcher (Interface for the Elderly)

This privacy policy describes how our application collects, uses, and protects user information. We created this application as a free software service for elderly people.

1. Collection and Use of Personal Data

Our application requires access to certain device features exclusively to provide the core functionality of the launcher:

  • SMS (READ_SMS, SEND_SMS): Used only to count the number of unread messages on the home screen and to send an emergency text message when the SOS mode is activated.
  • Calls and Contacts (READ_CALL_LOG, READ_CONTACTS): Used exclusively to display the number of missed calls on the home screen and to display the names of caregivers.
  • Camera (CAMERA): Used exclusively for the flashlight functionality.
  • Package Visibility (QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES): Used exclusively to display the full list of installed applications on the home screen and allow the user to launch them.
  • Notification Access (NotificationListenerService): Used only to intercept and display incoming notifications (like Viber or SMS) in an enlarged, accessible dialog on the home screen.

2. Third-Party Data Transfer

We do not collect, store, track, or transfer any of your personal data, message contents, or call history to external servers or third parties. All data operations occur locally on your device.

3. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may periodically update our Privacy Policy. Changes become effective immediately upon being posted on this page.
